Name of Quantlet: Network Centrality - USDC depeg

Published in: Network Centrality - USDC depeg

Description: The USDC depeg on March 11th 2023 is an interesting case study. The sudden collapse of the sillicon valley bank and the annoucement by Cricle that around 8% of the USDC reserves were affected triggered mass selloff events. In this courselet we study the few hours before and after the annoucement to highlight stress time onchain plumbing resilience and the rift between informed whales and regular users. We show how lower fee venues are progressively left for more costly altenatives as liquidity dries up and how users mostly rotated to USDT for fear of global market volatility.

Keywords: Graph centrality, ERC-20 transfer network, Stablecoin depeg

Author: Owen Chaffard
